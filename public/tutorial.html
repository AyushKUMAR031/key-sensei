Typing Tutorial

Master the art of touch typing with our guided lessons.

The Home Row

The home row is your anchor on the keyboard. Your fingers should always return here after striking any key. For your left hand, the home row keys are A, S, D, and F. For your right hand, they are J, K, L, and ;.

Feel for the small bumps on the F and J keys with your index fingers. These will help you find your position without looking.

Proper Posture

Good posture prevents strain and improves speed. Sit up straight with your feet flat on the floor. Your wrists should be in a neutral position, not bent or resting on any surface. Keep your elbows bent at a comfortable 90-degree angle.

Practice Makes Perfect

Consistency is key to improving your typing skills. Aim for accuracy first, then focus on speed. Dedicate at least 15 minutes each day to practice, and you'll see significant improvement over time.
