Typing Tutorial

The Home Row

The home row is the most important row on the keyboard. Your fingers should always return to the home row after typing any other key. The home row keys for your left hand are A, S, D, and F. For your right hand, they are J, K, L, and the semicolon (;).

Place your index fingers on the F and J keys. You should feel a small bump on each of these keys. This will help you find the home row without looking at the keyboard.

Proper Posture

Sit up straight with your feet flat on the floor. Your wrists should be straight and not resting on the keyboard or desk. Keep your elbows at a 90-degree angle.

Practice, Practice, Practice

The only way to get better at typing is to practice. Start by typing slowly and accurately. Speed will come with time. Try to type for at least 15 minutes every day.
